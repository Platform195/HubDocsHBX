title: Introduction
description: What the HBX Self Serve portal does and how this guide is organised.
---

HBX Self Serve lets you promote your hotel to high-intent B2B travel buyers across the HBX Group network — including Hotelbeds and Bedsonline — at the moment they're searching and booking. You choose your package, the markets you want to reach, and your budget, then launch and track campaigns yourself.

This guide walks through everything you need: [creating an account](/get-started/creating-an-account), [choosing a package](/packages/choosing-a-package), building a campaign through the [five-step wizard](/campaigns/create-campaign), and [submitting your order](/checkout/shopping-cart). A [glossary](/reference/glossary) explains the key terms — packages, source markets, sponsored listings, and add-ons.

<Note>
  The portal is fully self-service. You can launch a campaign in three steps —
  choose your package, set your campaign, and track your results — without
  speaking to a sales team.
</Note>
