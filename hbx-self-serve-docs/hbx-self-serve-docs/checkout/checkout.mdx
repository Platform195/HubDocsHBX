Checkout is the final step. You confirm who's being invoiced and where, agree to the terms, and submit. Payment is collected by invoice after submission, so there's nothing to pay in the portal itself.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your company details">
    Complete the **Details** section: **Company (legal entity name)**,
    **First name**, **Last name**, **Email address**, and **VAT Registration
    Number**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your registered business address">
    Choose your country from the **Registered business address** dropdown, then
    fill in **City** and **State, Province or Region** (both optional),
    **Address**, and **Zip/Post code**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree to the terms">
    Tick **I agree to HBX terms and conditions** and **I confirm I am an
    authorised representative of the above legal entity**. Both are required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the purchase">
    Review the **Summary** on the right, then click <Ui>Confirm Purchase</Ui>.
    Use <Ui>Edit cart</Ui> if you need to change anything first.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Payment will be collected via invoice after submission. Submitting the order
  does not take payment immediately.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Need a hand before submitting? The **Need help?** panel links you to one of our
  agents — see [Get help](/reference/support).
</Tip>
