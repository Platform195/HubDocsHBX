title: "Ad Formats"
---

Your package determines which ad formats you can run. There are two families: **sponsored listings**, which place your hotel within search results, and **display advertising**, which puts banner placements in front of travel buyers as they browse. Both put your property where booking decisions are made.

## Sponsored listings

Sponsored listings position your hotel within search results so you stand out exactly where booking decisions take place. There are two levels:

- **First Page** — your property features on the first page of search results through three rotating placements, keeping you visible while travel buyers compare options. Included with **Core\+**.
- **Top Position** — your hotel owns the first position on the first page of search results, front and centre at the moment of booking. Included with **Premium**.

## Display advertising

Display advertising shows banner placements across the network in your chosen source markets. This includes the Core Tile and Premium Banner placements bundled with the higher tiers, and can be extended to extra markets — see [Add-ons](/packages/add-ons).

<Note>
  The formats available to you depend on the package you select. Review what each tier includes on [Choosing a package](/packages/choosing-a-package).
</Note>
