Before you can build a campaign you need an HBX Self Serve account. If your organisation already has access, sign in with your email and password. If not, register a new account or request access.

## Registering a new account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up screen">
    Go to the portal and select the <Ui>Sign up</Ui> tab at the top of the
    login panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Provide your **Email**, a **Password**, and confirm the password. Tick
    **I have read the Terms &amp; Conditions** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register">
    Click <Ui>Register</Ui>. If your organisation isn't set up for self-serve
    access yet, use <Ui>Request access</Ui> instead and the team will be in touch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login screen">
    Go to the portal and make sure the <Ui>Login</Ui> tab is selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Enter your **Email** and **Password**. Tick <Ui>Keep me logged in</Ui> if
    you're on a trusted device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Click <Ui>Login</Ui>. You can also use <Ui>Sign in with …</Ui> if single
    sign-on is enabled for your organisation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Forgotten your password? Use <Ui>Forgot password?</Ui> on the login screen and
  follow the link sent to your inbox.
</Tip>
