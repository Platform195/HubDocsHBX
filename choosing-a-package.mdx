Packages are the starting point for any campaign. Each one bundles a set of placements that boost your property's visibility across the HBX Group network. Open **Packages** from the top navigation to compare the three tiers and the discount for longer bookings, then select one to begin building a campaign.

## The three packages

| Package | From | Best for | Headline inclusions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Core** | €1,000 / month | Starting to build brand awareness | Featured on the BOL and Hotelbeds landing page; one source market. Does not include sponsored listings. |
| **Core+** | €2,500 / month | Growing presence in key markets | Core Tile on BOL and Hotelbeds landing pages; first-page sponsored listing; four source markets. |
| **Premium** | €4,250 / month | Maximum reach and top position | Premium Banner; top-position sponsored listing; four source markets. |

<Note>
  Prices shown here are indicative. The exact price for your campaign is
  calculated from the package, markets and add-ons you choose, and is confirmed
  on the campaign **Summary** step and in your cart.
</Note>

## Timeline and discounts

At the top of the package selector you choose a **Timeline** for the campaign:

- **1 Month** — the standard monthly rate.
- **2+ Months** — longer bookings receive **25% off**.

The discount is applied automatically to qualifying campaigns and shown on the
**Summary** step and in your [Shopping Cart](/checkout/shopping-cart).

## Comparing packages

On the **Packages** page, use <Ui>Compare packages</Ui> to see a side-by-side
breakdown of what each tier includes before you commit. You can also change
package at the [Select package](/campaigns/select-package) step of the wizard.

<Tip>
  Not sure which tier to pick? Start with the visibility you need: **Core** for
  brand awareness, **Core+** to appear in first-page sponsored listings across
  several markets, or **Premium** to own the top position.
</Tip>

## What's next

When you've decided, select a package to open the campaign wizard — or read
about the [ad formats](/packages/ad-formats) and [add-ons](/packages/add-ons)
you can layer on top.
